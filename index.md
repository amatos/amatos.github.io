You probably want to go [here](/blog/)

Nihil sub sole novum nec valet quisquam dicere ecce hoc recens est iam enim praecessit in saeculis quae fuerunt ante nos.

Or, in more colloquial english:

Everything that happens
<br />has happened before;
<br />nothing is new,
<br />nothing under the sun.

Someone might say,
<br />“Here is something new!”
<br />But it happened before,
<br />long before we were born.
